Honeybees must gather nectar from 2 million flowers to make one pound of honey.
One bee has to fly about 90,000 miles to make one of pound of honey.
Honeybees communicate with each other by dancing.
The average bee will make only 1/12 teaspoon of honey in its lifetime.
A colony of bees consist of 20 to 60 thousand honey bees and one queen.
Worker honey bees are female, live for about 6 weeks and do all the work.
Honey is incredibly healthy and includes enzymes, vitamins and minerals.
Honeybees are more likely to pollinate a tobacco plant because they catch a buzz from the nicotine.
